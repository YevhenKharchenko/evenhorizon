GAME 4: HANGMAN

Hangman — guess wisely

Hangman invites you to guess a hidden word letter by letter, guided by a theme shown at the top. Every incorrect guess adds a new element to the drawing, increasing tension with each mistake. Classic hangman, but it actually helps with spelling and vocab without feeling like homework. It’s a classic format that remains engaging across all ages.

What It Strengthens:

  • Imagination and focus

  • Vocabulary recall

  • Logical deduction

  • Spelling accuracy
