ABOUT THE APP

A compact collection of logic and language

Instead of switching between separate apps, Puzzles with Words gathers four timeless word games in one place.

Each game works different parts of your brain - some test your memory, others challenge how quickly you spot patterns.

We kept the design super clean because honestly, there's nothing worse than fighting with a clunky app when you just want to play some word games.

It’s designed for players who enjoy quiet mental challenges without unnecessary noise.

GAME 1: ANAGRAMS

Anagrams — rearrange and rethink

Anagrams challenge you to rearrange letters and discover new words hidden within them. At first, it feels like a simple letter shuffle, but as combinations grow more complex, you need sharp focus and quick thinking. You'll get faster at spotting patterns the more you play - it's pretty satisfying when you start nailing them quickly. It also supports vocabulary development and memory retention without feeling like traditional study.

What It Trains:

  • Attention and concentration

  • Speed of thinking

  • Word recognition

  • Memory flexibility

GAME 2: REBUSES

Rebuses — decode the picture

Rebuses combine images, letters, numbers, and symbols to form riddles you must interpret correctly. These picture puzzles are wild - I've spent way too long staring at some of them before the answer finally clicks. You learn to connect visual hints with language, strengthening intuition and non-standard perception. It’s a playful but powerful way to expand vocabulary and sharpen logical reasoning.

What It Develops:

  • Logical thinking

  • Visual memory

  • Intuition

  • Creative interpretation

GAME 3: WORD SEARCH

Word Search — focus in the grid

Word Search presents a grid filled with letters, hiding words placed vertically, horizontally, diagonally, and even in reverse. Finding them requires patience, visual scanning, and steady attention. This mode encourages persistence and strengthens concentration skills over time. As word lists grow longer, so does the challenge — and the satisfaction of clearing them.

What It Improves:

  • Focus and perseverance

  • Vocabulary expansion

  • Pattern recognition

  • Patience under pressure

GAME 4: HANGMAN

Hangman — guess wisely

Hangman invites you to guess a hidden word letter by letter, guided by a theme shown at the top. Every incorrect guess adds a new element to the drawing, increasing tension with each mistake. Classic hangman, but it actually helps with spelling and vocab without feeling like homework. It’s a classic format that remains engaging across all ages.

What It Strengthens:

  • Imagination and focus

  • Vocabulary recall

  • Logical deduction

  • Spelling accuracy
